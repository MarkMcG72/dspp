Alzheimers Disease Detection from medical records and assessments

Logistic Regression model to predict Alzheimer's diagnosis
Data sourced from Kaggle.com

The dataset contained the mdeical records of 2149 patients of a mix of ages and evenly split by gender. Those Diagnosed with Alzheimer's represented the minority class.

K-Fold Cross Validationg

Since a single, realatively small dataset was available, K-Fold Cross Validation was used to check how this model might generalise.

Confusion Matrix
Prior to using SMOTE

Running Logisitic Regression against the original data resulted in sub-par performance. In particular the Recall score was low for this scenario.

Confusion Matrix
After SMOTE applied

After synthetically boosting the minority class, the performance across all metrics (including Recall) is improved.
